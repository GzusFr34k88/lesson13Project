Welcome to the Game

Mobile:

Up: swipe up
Down: swipe down
Left: swipe left
Right: swipe right

Computer:

Up: Use up arrow
Down: Use down arrow
Left: Use left arrow
Right: Use right arrow

Instructions

You get five lives to get the highest score you can. Blue gems are worth 25 points, green gems are worth 50 points, and yellow gems are worth 100 points. Don't get hit by a bug and don't touch the water. Good luck!
